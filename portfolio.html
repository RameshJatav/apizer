About Me

Hello! I'm Ramesh Jatav from Malakhera, Alwar (Rajasthan), belonging to a middle‑class family.

I completed my 10th in 2019 and 12th in 2022 from Government Senior Secondary School, Kerwawal, Alwar. In 2023, I earned an Advance Diploma in Web Development Professional (Grade A) from DIIT Educom, Alwar, where I built strong foundations in MySQL, JavaScript, PHP, Laravel, and backend architecture.

From August 2023 to November 2024, I worked at Zulu IT Solutions (ZITS) as a Backend Developer. Over 14 months, I built high‑quality RESTful APIs using Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, and JWT authentication. I developed e‑commerce platforms with Razorpay payments, secure admin dashboards, and a hotel management system for Rambihari Palace.

After a short learning break focused on Sequelize ORM, advanced MySQL, and Socket.io, I joined the “Airtel Distributor” project from February to July 2025. I built a secure admin panel handling transactions, SMS notifications, balance tracking, and role‑based access with JWT.

I’m currently working as a dedicated Node.js Backend Developer, committed to writing clean, efficient, and secure server‑side code.
