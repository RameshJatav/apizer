About Me

Hello! I'm Ramesh Jatav, a passionate backend developer from Malakhera, Alwar, Rajasthan. Coming from a humble middle-class family, I completed my 10th in 2019 and 12th in 2022 from Government Senior Secondary School, Kerwawal.

In 2023, I earned an Advance Diploma in Web Development (ADWP) from DIIT Educom, Alwar, where I gained skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Laravel, MySQL, and more — passing with Grade A.

I began my professional career as a backend developer at Zulu IT Solutions (ZITS) (Aug 2023 – Nov 2024), where I spent 14 months building secure, scalable APIs using Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Sequelize, and JWT authentication. I created admin dashboards, payment gateway integrations (Razorpay), and even managed hotel management systems.

After a brief 2-month learning break, I was hired in Feb 2025 for a project titled "Airtel Distributor" – a role-based admin panel managing mobile shop transactions, SMS notifications, and financial logs, built with Node.js, Sequelize ORM, and JWT-based authorization.

I’m currently working as a dedicated Node.js Backend Developer with a focus on clean code, performance, and real-world problem solving.
